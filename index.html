SODIA

In a time of blood and fire, the kingdom of Sodia is about to be overrun by the worst kind of enemies.

You are the guardian of the village, if you fall the kingdom will fall with you.

Mission

  • • You have to defend your kingdom by killing the enemies.
  • • If the enemies touches you, you die.
  • • When you kill an enemy, you earn 10 or 20 points, depending on the type of enemy killed.
  • • when you reach 1000 points the worst enemy called BabyDragon appears and you have to kill it to win the game.

    Instruction

  • • Press the start button to play
  • • Press Left/ Right/ Up/ Down to control the player.
  • • Press space to shoot.
