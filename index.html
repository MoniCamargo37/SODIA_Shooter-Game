Warrior Guard

2000 thousand years ago, a kingdom was invaded by the worst enemies. They took advantage that the guardian warrior was not in his tower, for some reason, and invaded the kingdom. It was the guardian's duty to defend his kingdom, but unfortunately he failed. Now the kingdom will have another chance to win the battle because you will be the guardian.

Are you ready to save the kingdom?

Mission

  • You have to defend your kingdom by killing the enemies.
  • If the enemies touches you, you die.
  • When you kill an enemy, you earn 10 or 20 points, depending on the type of enemy killed.
  • when you get 1000 points the worst enemy called BabyDragon appears and you will have the mission to kill it, apart from killing the enemies at the same time.

    • Instruction

  • Press the start button to play
  • Press Left/ Right/ Up/ Down to control the player.
  • Press space to shoot.
